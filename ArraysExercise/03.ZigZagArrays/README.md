1. 3.Zig-Zag Arrays

Write a program which creates 2 arrays. You will be given an integer **n**. On the next **n** lines you get 2 integers. Form 2 arrays as shown below.

### Examples

| **Input** | **Output** |
| --- | --- |
| 41591031814120 | 1 10 31 205 9 81 41 |
| 280 2331 19 | 80 1923 31 |
